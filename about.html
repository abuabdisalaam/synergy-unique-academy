Our Journey

📅 2019

Synergy Unique Academy began with the College Section, enrolling only six pioneering students with a team of five dedicated staff members.

📅 2020

Despite the COVID-19 pandemic, the school resumed operations and established the Primary Section, laying the foundation for future growth.

📅 Today

The Academy has grown to over 200 learners and 20 qualified teachers, with outstanding results in BECE, WAEC and UTME examinations.

Our Vision

To be a world-class centre of learning that raises God-conscious, morally upright, innovative, and globally competitive leaders who positively transform their communities and the world.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing sound education without compromise by integrating academic excellence, Islamic values, character development, leadership training, innovation, technology, creativity, and life skills in a safe, nurturing, and inspiring environment, thereby equipping every learner to excel in this world and attain success in the Hereafter.
